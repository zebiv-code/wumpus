Hunt the Wumpus

Hunt the Wumpus

By Gregory Yob, 1973

The Cave

The Wumpus lives in a cave of 20 rooms shaped like an icosahedron. Each room connects to 3 others.

Hazards

Wumpus — Usually asleep. If you enter his room, he wakes and eats you. If you shoot and miss, he may move to your room.
Pits — Two rooms have bottomless pits. Fall in and you lose.
Bats — Two rooms have super bats that carry you to a random room.

Warnings

When one room away from a hazard:
Wumpus: "I smell a Wumpus!"
Pit: "I feel a draft!"
Bats: "Bats nearby!"

Actions

Move — Click a blue adjacent room to move there.
Shoot — You have 5 crooked arrows. Click Shoot, then click rooms to set the arrow's path (1-5 rooms). If no tunnel exists, the arrow flies randomly. Hit the Wumpus to win. Hit yourself to lose. Run out of arrows and you lose.
